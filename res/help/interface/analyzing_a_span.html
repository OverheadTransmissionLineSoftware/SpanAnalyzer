Analyzing a Span

Activating a Span

A span can be activated by double clicking the span, or by right clicking and selecting 'Activate'. The span is activated when the font turns bold.

The Analysis

Once a span is activated, it will trigger an analysis. The analysis will solve for every combination of weathercase and stretch condition. A solution entails:

  • Unloading the span from the constraint to the target weathercase/condition
  • Reloading the span from the constraint to the target weathercase/condition

The analysis usually finishes quickly on modern computer hardware. SpanAnalyzer also speeds things along by using all of the available CPU cores.

Deactivating a Span

A span can be deactivated to clear the analysis results. Right click on the span, and select 'Deactivate'.
